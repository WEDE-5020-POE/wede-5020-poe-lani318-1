## Project Overview
Organisation overview
Precision Auto Care is small automotive repair business that was started in 2019 in Johannesburg. It began as a local workshop focused on helping people fix and maintain their vehicles at affordable prices. Over time, the business has grown and build a good reputation in the community for being reliable and providing quality service.
Mission statement
The mission for the business is to provide professional, reliable, and affordable automotive repair and maintenance services that ensures the safety of the vehicle, how the car performs, and customer satisfaction. Precise Auto Care is committed to deliver quality workmanship, using effective diagnostic solution, and building long-term relationships with customers through honest service, trust, and excellence. 
Vision Statement
Our vision is to grow Precision Auto Care into a trusted and respected automotive repair business that people can rely on for quality service and honest workmanship. We want to be known not only for fixing vehicles well, but also for putting customers first and building lasting relationships based on trust. As the business grows, we aim to keep improving by using modern tools, maintaining high service standards, and expecting our reach while continuing to provide dependable repairs that keep our customers safe on the road.  

## Website Goals and Objectives
The main purpose of creating a website for precision Auto Care is to help the business reach more customers and make it easier for people to find its services online. Instead of replying only on walk in customers, the website will allow people to learn about the services offered, check basic information, and even book appointments online without having to come to the workshop. It will also help build trust by showing customers reviews and quality of the after wok. Another important goal is to increase enquiries and bookings through the website. Things like checking how many people visited the website and how many bookings were made in order to see if the website is working.
